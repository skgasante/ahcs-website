About Us

A School Where Every Child is Seen and Known

Annan House Community School was founded on a simple conviction: that every child deserves an education that sees them as a whole person. We are not just teaching subjects. We are shaping character, building confidence, and preparing young people for a world that needs their best.

Located in the heart of Airport Residential, Accra, we serve families from all walks of life with a consistent commitment to quality, warmth, and excellence.

Excellence in Learning

We hold high academic standards while making learning enjoyable and meaningful.

Inclusive Community

Every child belongs here. We celebrate difference and build unity.

Partnerships with Parents

Strong home-school relationships are central to how we operate.

We offer a rich, Cambridge International Curriculum-based programme from our earliest learners through to Cambridge Lower Secondary (Year 9).
